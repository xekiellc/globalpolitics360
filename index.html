About GP360

Global Politics 360 is an independent news and podcast aggregator dedicated to voices that challenge mainstream narratives. We curate articles, investigations, analysis, podcast episodes, and videos from a hand-selected list of journalists, commentators, and podcasters — updated every three hours.

This site does not produce original reporting. We surface and organize content so you can stay informed without the noise.

How It Works

Every three hours, an automated system fetches articles, podcast episodes, and videos from curated RSS feeds. Claude AI writes one-sentence teasers for each news article, filters the Tech Abundance feed for optimism, and runs guest detection across all podcast episodes and videos.
