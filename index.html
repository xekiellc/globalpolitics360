About GP360

Global Politics 360 is an independent news and podcast aggregator dedicated to voices that challenge mainstream narratives. We curate articles, investigations, analysis, and podcast episodes from a hand-selected list of journalists, commentators, and podcasters — updated every three hours.

This site does not produce original reporting. We surface and organize content so you can stay informed without the noise.

Tech Abundance — Editorial Philosophy

Every article in the Tech Abundance tab passes through an AI sentiment filter. Only optimistic, builder-focused, progress-positive content makes it through. Doom framing, regulation panic, and tech criticism are dropped automatically. The filter also explicitly surfaces positive coverage of key builders: Elon Musk, Palmer Luckey, Alex Karp, Joe Lonsdale, Jensen Huang, Chamath, Marc Andreessen, David Sacks, Balaji Srinivasan, and Peter Diamandis.

Guest Detection

GP360 automatically scans every podcast episode for mentions of curated voices. When someone appears as a guest on any tracked show, that episode is tagged and surfaced — so you never miss an appearance.

How It Works

Every three hours, an automated system fetches articles and podcast episodes from curated RSS feeds. Claude AI writes one-sentence teasers for each news article, filters the Tech Abundance feed for optimism, and runs guest detection across all podcast episodes.
